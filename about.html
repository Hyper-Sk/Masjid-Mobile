About masjid e miskeen shah

Welcome to Miskeen Shah Masjid

Miskeen Shah Masjid serves as the central hub for the Muslims in the area. We have a thriving and ever-growing community surrounding the Masjid that participates daily in the Masjid activities. Whether you are a new Muslim, a non-Muslim or have always been a Muslim, we invite you to come to the Masjid to participate in the Masjid activities and learn more about Islam.
